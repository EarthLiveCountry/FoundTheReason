FoundTheReason

Non-admin BSOD risk checker for Windows

Overview

FoundTheReason scans your PC for potential issues that may lead to system crashes, including disk space, RAM, CPU load, and application errors. It helps you identify risks and provides suggestions to keep your system stable.
